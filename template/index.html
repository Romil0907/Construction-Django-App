Introduction

Bhugan Infracon Private limited previously known as Bhugan Engineers is Goverment Approved contractor and project developer. Our strength lies in our ability to develop qualitative, technically superior and timely execution of projects for our clients. We have been operating since 1996, and our unique combination of local knowledge and extensive experience has made us the nation's contractor of choice.

We focus on a number of specific construction segments:
➤ Bridge Construction
➤ Roads & Highway Projects
➤ Earthwork
➤ Irrigation and Dams
➤ Environmental Landfill and Vermi Compost Projects
➤ Water Supply and Sewerage Projects
➤ Residential and Industrial Building Projects
➤ BOT Based Projects.

BACKGROUND AND HISTORY

Bhugan Infracon Private Limited is a civil engineering company of national repute and firmly established in the vibrant state of Gujarat. The company is registered in the highest category AA class for the execution of R & B Division. With a staff of more than 500 employees and having successfully completed over 20 various types of projects we are truly a Key player in performing and developing infrastructure all over Gujarat. We started in 1996 as a civil engineering company under the name of Bhugan Engineers. With what started with an initial investment of Rs 5 Lac in the year 1996 and working as subcontractor for various Reputed Goverment Approved Contractors under the leadership of Shri Ganeshbhai N. Patel has now grown to an organization of national repute with an annual turnover of over 198.80 Crore in the year 2018-19. Recently in 2013-14 Bhugan Engineers was merge and now known’s as Bhugan Infracon Private limited for future development. Our services have enabled our clients to undertake large turnkey projects, combined with our start of art technology, delivering and managing processes to reduce timescales, cost and disruption.
