WATER & ENVIRONMENT

The economy of our nation thrives on agriculture, which is the main source of livelihood for millions of population which is crucial to the growth of our country. Seamless distribution of precious water to irrigate agricultural lands is the fundamental essence for a successful and sustaining agrarian economy. Going beyond the crucial drenching of the farm lands, water is also needed to quench the thirst of millions of our countrymen. Bhugan Infracon Private Limited, inspired by the vision of its Founder-Chairman Sri Ganeshbhai N. Patel, has focused on undertaking major and minor irrigation projects for sustained rural development.

Bhugan Infracon's irrigation projects focus on better utilization of water, building quality irrigation infrastructure that includes dams, weirs, lift irrigation schemes and canal networks. The company has partnered with public bodies in designing and creating the needful irrigation infrastructure across India.
