Special Message from the Managing Director


{% comment %} class="col-md-6" {% endcomment %}

We started on this journey of Bhugan Infracon Private Limited in 1996 with a small team and a big dream – ‘Be recognized as the most reliable engineering and Infrastructure Company in India’. In our 24 year journey, there have been tremendous highs and lows, extreme challenges and great learnings. But all the while, the dream has persisted.

Over the past couple of years, Bhugan Infracon Private Limited has seen a huge growth and transformation unlike any since our inception. Our verticals have grown, we have been awarded bigger and better projects, our geographical span has expanded and our teams have grown manifold. We have evolved from a small local construction set up, to a recognized and respected infrastructure construction and development company in India.

Our journey has been made possible because of the blessing and good wishes of all our employees, their families and our various stakeholders. We will continue to evolve, transform and grow and while we do, we hope to have your continued support and good wishes.

➤ Ganeshbhai N. Patel
